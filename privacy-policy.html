Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our local photo editing application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is fully secure. Below is our privacy policy:

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Our application is designed to run entirely on your local device, with all photo editing operations processed on your device. Your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. No Login Required

Our application does not require any form of login or registration. You can use all features without providing any personal information.

3. Internet Connection for Payments and Receipt Verification Only

The application only requires an internet connection when you make an official payment. This connection is used solely to process the payment and verify the receipt with our server. We do not collect or store any payment information; the verification process ensures that your transaction is valid and secure.

4. Third-Party Services

For payment processing, we may use third-party service providers. These providers may collect information necessary to complete the transaction, but this information is not stored or processed by us. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these providers for more details on how they handle your data.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.
